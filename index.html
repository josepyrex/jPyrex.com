About Me

I am a native of Venezuela who currently resides in New York City. My passion for technology and website design, runs deep. I also hold an appreciation for all various type of music and audio equipment.

Presently, I am engaged in a self-taught pursuit to develop iPhone applications using SwiftUI and to construct websites utilizing Ruby On Rails. The year 2020's quarantine period became the starting point for my passion for website development, with my initial work on WordPress, followed by the acquisition of skills in HTML, CSS, and JS. In addition, I possess experience in website hosting, which extends to proficiency in resolving DNS and database issues through the use of SQL and PHP.

I possess a great fondness for people and have devoted six years to the provision of customer service. My priority has always been to assist clients in solving their problems and provide support as needed.
